## Original

I LOVE THIS TINY GREEN ISLAND
SURROUNDED BY THE SEA.
TOUCHED BY THE SEA,
DECORATED BY THE SEA.
I LOVE THIS TINY GREEN ISLAND
SURROUNDED BY THE SEA.

AM I A SECLUDED FIGURE
IN THE VAST A LITTLE A MEAGER?
NO, NO, NO, NO, I AM NOT ALONE.
THE GREAT IS WITH ME.
THE GREAT IS WITH ME.
